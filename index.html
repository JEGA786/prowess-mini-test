Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards 2024

The Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards were established in 2015 to celebrate the outstanding contributions of authors who inspire and enrich the literary landscape. Each year, these prestigious awards recognize the dedication, creativity, and passion that authors bring to their craft.

The 2024 awards continue this cherished tradition, and it is with immense pride that we acknowledge the extraordinary efforts of our nominees and winners. This year, we had an impressive pool of talented authors who have pushed the boundaries of storytelling and engaged readers with their unique voices.

The aim of the awards

The Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards celebrate exceptional talent in the literary community by honoring outstanding works across various genres. These awards encourage literary excellence and promote diverse voices, providing a platform for authors to gain visibility and recognition for their contributions.

Aim of the awards

The Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards celebrate exceptional talent and creativity in the literary community.

  • Encourage Literary Excellence: Inspire authors to pursue their passion and achieve excellence in their craft.
  • Promote Diverse Voices: Highlight a range of genres and perspectives, fostering inclusivity in the literary world.
  • Support Author Recognition: Provide a platform for authors to gain visibility and acknowledgment for their contributions to literature.

These awards honor individual achievements while enriching and fostering the growth of the literary community as a whole.

Meet Our 2024 Award Winners

We are proud to introduce the winners of the Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards 2024. These remarkable authors have captivated readers with their compelling stories, unique voices, and exceptional writing. Each of them has pushed the boundaries of creativity and contributed significantly to the literary world.

R.V. Reddy - 1st Place

Book Title : Go Bharat

R.V. Reddy is a distinguished advocate practicing at the Supreme Court of India, recognized for his passion for yoga, literature, and nature. He has served as Past President of both Lions and Rotary clubs and is currently the President of the Association of British Scholars, drawing inspiration from his father, an award-winning short story writer.

He completed a diploma in Creative Writing from IGNOU in 1989, honing his craft under the mentorship of esteemed figures like Prof. Shiv K. Kumar and Geethika Saigal. An avid reader, he is inspired by works such as Stephen King’s On Writing and remains a devoted fan of Ruskin Bond, reflecting a blend of creativity and discipline in all his endeavors.

Dr.Kumaresan - 2nd Place

Book Title : Umar For Puberphonia

Dr. Muthiah Kumaresan is a renowned ENT surgeon, President of the Paediatric ENT Association of India, and a retired Civil Surgeon. He has served as a specialist for Indian Airlines and the International Airport Authority, and is a distinguished member of the Pulitzers Society, USA, with numerous national and state awards to his credit.

He earned his MBBS, DLO, and M.S. (ENT) from Madras Medical College in 1974 and developed an innovative evaluation model for puberphonia, rooted in responsive constructivism. Dr. Kumaresan has contributed extensively to community health through voice care camps, 36 published books, articles, and social media outreach.

Cina KS - 3nd Place

Book Title : Winter Hues

Cina K S , originally from Kerala and now settled in Bangalore, is a dynamic professional with a unique career journey. She transitioned from a background in Electrical and Electronics Engineering to over 23 years of experience in the animation and VFX industry, making significant contributions to both Hollywood and Indian cinema. Her technical expertise and leadership have been instrumental in various high-profile international projects.

Beyond her work in animation and VFX, Cina is an award-winning screenplay writer and artist, showcasing her creativity across various mediums. Her passion for classical dance offers her both inspiration and solace. Cina's diverse talents beautifully blend technical expertise with artistic expression, making her a truly multifaceted personality.

Arul Pandi - 4th Place

Book Title : Opt To Options Trading After Reading These Pages

Mr. P. Arul Pandi is a seasoned professional with over 30 years of experience in the financial sector, having served as an executive at a leading Government of India-owned bank. His deep passion for the financial markets and mastery of various technical trading tools have made him a respected figure in the trading community.

Inspired by a desire to share his knowledge, Mr. Pandi transitioned into writing, with his debut book Don't Trade Before Learning These 14 Candlestick Patterns becoming a bestseller on Amazon. His books provide practical, accessible insights for traders of all levels, and he actively conducts workshops and seminars to guide others in enhancing their trading skills. With a commitment to empowering traders, Mr. P. Arul Pandi continues to inspire individuals through his writings and teachings, offering them the tools and confidence to navigate the complexities of financial markets.

Bijoy Chandra Ghosh - 5th Place

Book Title : Principles and Practices of Organic Farming and Quality Food Production

Dr. Bijoy Chandra Ghosh, a retired professor from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, has had a distinguished career in agricultural research. His pioneering work has significantly advanced the understanding of organic farming and soil health, particularly highlighting the detrimental effects of chemical farming on the environment. With 185 research papers published in national and international journals, he has made a substantial contribution to sustainable agriculture.

Dr. Ghosh has supervised 18 Ph.D. students and developed numerous entrepreneurs in composting and vermicomposting. His expertise spans tea cultivation, rooftop gardening, and crop diversification, and he has traveled extensively to share his knowledge with the global agricultural community.

Terms and Conditions & Award FAQs

1. The aim of the awards

The Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards celebrate exceptional talent and creativity in the literary community.

2. Submission Guidelines

Entries must be submitted within the specified deadline to be considered for the awards. Each author can submit multiple books; however, each book must be submitted separately. All entries must be complete, including the submission of all required materials and information.

3. Judging Process

The judging panel consists of experienced professionals in the publishing industry. Books will be evaluated based on criteria such as originality, writing quality, creativity, and overall impact. The judges' decisions are final and cannot be challenged or appealed.

4. Nomination and Selection

Shortlisted nominees will be announced before the awards ceremony. Winners will be selected from the pool of nominees and announced during the awards event. Prowess Publishing reserves the right to withhold any award category if the entries do not meet the required standards.

5. Prizes and Benefits

Winners will receive a certificate, trophy, and promotional benefits, as specified by Prowess Publishing. Prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash or other products.

1. Who is eligible to participate in the Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards?

The awards are open to authors who have published their books with Prowess Publishing. Entries must be original works by the participating author, and the books should have been published within the specified eligibility period.

2. How can I submit my book for the awards?

To submit your book, visit the official Prowess Publishing Best Author Awards submission page, fill out the entry form, and upload a digital copy of your book along with the required details.

3. When will the winners be announced?

The winners will be announced during the awards ceremony, which will be held at a date specified on the Prowess Publishing website. Shortlisted authors will be notified in advance.

4. Can I enter multiple books?

Yes, authors may submit multiple books. However, each book must be entered separately with individual entry forms and submission fees (if applicable).

5. How are the winners selected?

The judging panel evaluates all entries based on established criteria, including originality, writing quality, and overall impact. The process is thorough, and decisions made by the judges are final.
